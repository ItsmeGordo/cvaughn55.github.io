About Me

I'm Christian Vaughn, a guy who occasionally makes video games and other random programs.

I have always been interested in game development and programming, but didnt start messing around with that stuff till around 2013. I found a game engine called Stencyl which is a neat game engine for beginers since it has easy to understand block code, and a great community filled with demos and tutorials

Since then I have been Messing around with various game ideas in Stencyl and Unity while currently persuing a Computer Science degree, and also doing various other programming projects. I hope to someday make a larger scale game that is more fleshed out and polished than the flash games I have made in the past.
